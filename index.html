1. Balanced Forces & Equilibrium

When the forces acting on an object are equal in size and opposite in direction, they are balanced. The object is in equilibrium and the forces cancel each other out.

Check the State:

When forces are balanced, a stationary object will remain and a moving object will continue at a speed.

3. Unbalanced Forces

When the forces acting on an object are not equal, they are unbalanced. The overall force acting on the object is the Resultant Force.
